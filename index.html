Bio

For nearly two decades I have dedicated my life to putting smiles on faces. The sentimental tears from the restored beauty of great-grandmother's heirloom bracelet, the glow of a new bride as she looks at her new engagement ring, and the excitement of earrings or a necklace to celebrate a special occasion have all impacted me as much as my workmanship has affected them. Now I wish to change my focus to a greater scope.

Jewelry only impacts a small percentage of the population at any given time. Websites are a necessity. As the human race becomes more reliant on technology, developers who can meld functionality with a pleasing interface will be in demand. But how am I qualified to offer this? How could my experience as a jeweler translate to web development?
